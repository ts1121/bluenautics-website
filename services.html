Our Services

Comprehensive sourcing and business development solutions connecting North America with Bangladesh

Apparel & RMG Sourcing

Complete sourcing solutions for ready-made garments, connecting you with Bangladesh's world-class apparel manufacturing network

Design & Development

We coordinate with our partner factories to bring your vision to life, from initial sketches to tech packs and sample development. Our network includes facilities equipped with in-house design teams capable of translating concepts into production-ready specifications.

Intelligent Merchandising & Costing

Strategic sourcing decisions backed by comprehensive cost analysis. We work with partners to provide detailed costing breakdowns, fabric and trim recommendations, and value engineering opportunities to optimize your margins while maintaining quality standards.

Fabric & Trim Sourcing

Access to Bangladesh's extensive textile ecosystem. Through our partner network, we facilitate sourcing of knits, wovens, and specialized fabrics from certified mills, along with coordinated trim packages including buttons, zippers, labels, and packaging materials.

Smart Production Placement

Strategic factory selection based on your specific requirements. We match your orders with appropriate manufacturing partners based on product category, volume, quality level, and timeline requirements, ensuring optimal production placement within our vetted network.

Quality Assurance & Control

Multi-stage quality oversight throughout the production process. We coordinate with partner facilities to implement quality checkpoints from fabric inspection through in-line production monitoring and final pre-shipment inspections, ensuring products meet your specifications.

Logistics Coordination

End-to-end shipment management from factory to your warehouse. We coordinate with logistics partners to handle documentation, customs compliance, freight forwarding, and delivery tracking, ensuring on-time arrival of your goods to destinations across the United States and Canada.
